🫁 Asthma Safety
Read before every session
💨

Always have your inhaler nearby before starting.

🌡️

Work out in a warm, indoor space — cold air triggers attacks.

🛑

Stop immediately if you feel chest tightness, wheezing, or dizziness.

🔥

Never skip your warm-up — it primes your airways for exercise.

😮‍💨

Breathe through your nose when possible to warm and filter air.

💪

Upper body work raises breathing rate less than cardio — still stay controlled.
